Vanilla Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 225g soft butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 375g white flour
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 240ml milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C
  2. Add the soft butter, white & brown sugar into a bowl and mix until creamy.
  3. Add the eggs one by one and the vanilla extract to the mixture.
  4. After mixing, add the flour and the baking powder and fold it in.
  5. Mix in the milk.
  6. Fill the batter into muffin tins.
  7. Bake for 40minutes.
  8. After baking let them fully cool.

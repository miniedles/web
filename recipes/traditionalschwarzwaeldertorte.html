Traditional Schwarzwaelder Cake

Ingredients:

Cake Batter

  • 100g butter
  • 300g sugar
  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 16g baking powder
  • 300g white flour
  • 50g cacao powder

Cherry Filling

  • 400g cherries
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 50ml water
  • 50g cornstarch

Cherry Sirup

  • 100g cherries>
  • 50ml water
  • 2tsp lemon juice
  • 20g sugar (adjust based how sweet you want it)

Frosting

  • 1'000ml whole cream
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 1tsp gelatin
  • 1 1/2tsp cold water

Decoration

  • ~300g chocolate sprinkles

    • Steps:

      Cherry Sirup

    1. Remove the stones from the cherries and add them to a pot.
    2. Also add the water, lemon juice and sugar to it and let it come to a boil, after that let it simmer for around 5mins.
    3. Take the pot off the heat and filter it through so the cherries are gone.
    4. Fill the syrup into a bowl or a bottle and let it fully cool down and put it into the fridge.

      5. Cherry Filling

    5. Remove the stones from the cherries and cut them in half. Add them into a pot.
    6. Add the sugar and the water into the pot and let it come to a boil.
    7. After it starts to boil, put down the heat and let it simmer for a few minutes.
    8. Then add the cornstarch mixed with a bit of water so it doesn't clump, then stir instantly so it thickens.
    9. Remove from the heat and let it cool down in a bowl. Put it in the fridge for now.

      10. Cake Batter

    10. Add the sugar, eggs and the salt into a bowl and mix it for about 10mins until it's very thick.
    11. Preheat oven to 175°C
    12. Then add the soft butter and mix it into the mixture.
    13. Add the flour, baking powder and cocoa powder in the mixture (sieve it) and fold it in.
    14. Then add the dough into two different baking tins and bake for around 45minutes.
    15. After baking, let it fully cool and cut into 2 parts each, then wrap into foil and put it in the fridge.

      16. Frosting

    16. Sprinkle the gelatin over the water and let it bloom for about 5 minutes.
    17. Melt the gelatin for about 5 seconds in the microwave until fully melted.
    18. In a mixing bowl add the heavy cream and mix until foamy.
    19. Add the mascarpone into the mixture and mix for a bit more.
    20. On low speed, drizzle in your gelatin and continue mixing until the peaks are holding their shape.
    21. Put the frosting into a bowl and put it in the fridge.

      22. Cake Assemble and Decoration

    22. After all these steps, we can assemble and decorate the cake.
    23. Place one of the cake layers down and slightly soak it with the cherry syrup.
    24. Then take your frosting and build walls on top of the layer so the cherry filling will not leak.
    25. Take the cherry filling and spread it on top of the cake layer.
    26. Then place another layer on top and repeat these steps until you don't have any more cake layers.
    27. If you've reached the top one you can use your frosting to spread it on top and around the cake.
    28. Then you can follow up with chocolate sprinkles and spread them around and on top of the cake.
    29. For some more decoration, you can also place cherries on top of the cake!

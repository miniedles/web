Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients:

  • 225g soft butter
  • 100g white sugar
  • 130g brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 250g white flour
  • 1/2tsp baking powder
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 150g chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Add the soft butter, white & brown sugar into a bowl and mix until creamy.
  3. Add the egg and the vanilla extract to the mixture.
  4. After mixing, add the flour, baking powder & baking soda and fold it in.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Shape them into equally large spheres and put them on a baking tray.
  7. Bake for 13minutes until golden brown and then let them fully cool.
